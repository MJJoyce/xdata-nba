Holiday leads 76ers over Bobcats, 100-92 By ROB MAADDI Posted Mar 31 2013 12:08AM PHILADELPHIA (AP)  Jrue Holiday scored 10 straight points down the stretch, Evan Turner finished with 22 and the Philadelphia 76ers beat the Charlotte Bobcats 100-92 Saturday night. After going 1 for 9 in the first three quarters, Holiday got 11 of his 14 points in the fourth to help the Sixers win for the seventh time in 11 games. Spencer Hawes had 19 points and Thaddeus Young and Damien Wilkins each scored 15 for Philadelphia. Michael Kidd-Gilchrist had 21 for Charlotte, which has the worst record in the NBA. Gerald Henderson, who had 35 and 34 in his previous two games, finished with 19. Copyright 2013 by STATS LLC and Associated Press.
