
 Bargnani scores 26, Raptors beat Rockets 106-96 Posted Nov 19 2010 9:46PM TORONTO(AP) Andrea Bargnani scored 26 points, Linas Kleiza had 17 and the Toronto Raptors beat the Houston Rockets 106-96 Friday night to win consecutive games for the first time this season. DeMar DeRozan added 15 points, Sonny Weems had 13 and Amir Johnson had 10 points and eight rebounds for the Raptors. Jose Calderon had 10 points and seven assists, Jarrett Jack had eight assists and Reggie Evans had nine rebounds. Kevin Martin matched his season high with 31 points, Luis Scola had 19 and Shane Battier 14 as Houston lost its third straight and dropped to 3-9. Copyright 2010 by STATS LLC and Associated Press.
